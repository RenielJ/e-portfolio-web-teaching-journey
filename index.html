Content Knowledge and Pedagogy

1st Domain

Group presentations and reporting in front of a class can help establish teaching skills and speaking confidence. The act of teaching is the core of the learning process, and it motivates me to improve myself as an educator.

Deep knowledge about the subject matter is always essential when explaining the main idea of a topic. Inclusive teaching practices are one of my valuable approaches, as learners have diverse their needs and motivations.

  • Domain: First
  • Content: The teaching strategy

Learning Environment

2nd Domain

Dividing students into sections allows for better classroom management, enhances the learning experience, improves effective teaching, and provides students with the necessary support for their learning growth.

During my teaching internship, teaching practices allowed me to grow my personality and develop my thoughts about different approaches for each section. While facilitating and teaching are distinct approaches, an educator should always consider the pedagogy of both when planning instruction.

  • Domain: Second
  • Content: The classroom and school environment

Diversity of Learners

3rd Domain

Every learner has different life experiences and living environments, making teaching diversity one of the most essential skills for educators to develop in the classroom. Learners have diverse thoughts and priorities: some are content with their current knowledge, some are eager to learn new things, and many high school students are primarily focused on grades.

During my internship, I observed that while most students prioritized taking notes for grades and exam review, some did not take notes throughout the entire quarter. As a facilitator, I treat all students equally and provide grades based on their performance throughout the quarter.

  • Domain: Third
  • Content: The differences of learners

Curriculum and Planning

4th Domain

Preparation is the most crucial aspect of effective classroom teaching. I used to wonder why teachers create lesson plans for lectures, but the answer lies in being prepared for any situation that may arise in the classroom. Classroom management is a vital skill that educators must possess.

During my internship, I observed that reviewing to lesson plan resulted in best flow of classroom settings and discussion, from the opening prayer and greetings to the assessment and evaluation.

  • Domain: Forth
  • Content: The Blueprint

Assessment and Reporting

5th Domain

Evaluation should need the materials for assist the learners from their instruction during assessment. Based on my experience in my internship, most of my activities I created always have rubrics to follow for the scoring of their activities. Rubrics helps learners to follow the procedure and evaluate their own works to be more consistent and precise follow the instruction.

In addition, learners need a guidance that's why when creating instructions, it needs to be more precisely, clear, and simple.

  • Domain: Fifth
  • Content: The Evaluation Strategy
